Project Summary¶

“TESS Data for Asteroseismology” (or TDA)¶

To join the group go to tasoc.dk and sign up for WG0, you can then via our wiki read more about the work of the group and how to contribute.

If you have any questions about the group, the work, or how to contribute, do not hesitate to contact the working group chairs: Mikkel Lund and Rasmus Handberg.

Goals¶

  • Do basic calibrations of raw data
  • Define stellar apertures and extract flux
  • Correct light curves for instrumental features
  • Make stellar classification

Inputs¶

  • Pixel data from TESS
  • Good ideas and hard work from the working group

Methodology¶

  • Relevant articles include:
    • Ricker et al. (2016, Proc of the SPIE)
    • Lund et al. (2015, ApJ 806, 30)
